Power Bank Knowledge
  • Do I need to recharge the Power bank before the first time use?
    Yes, for maximum battery capacity, please recharge it till the LED indicator is off .
  • How can I tell the charging status ?
    Users can check it by the color of LED indicator. “Green light” means the status of discharging form the power bank(charging amobile device) while “red light “means the status of charging power bank.
  • How to recharge a power bank?
    When recharging the power bank ,please follow the steps.
    First , please plug the mini-USB connector into the “IN” port of the power bank , and then the other connector into the computer or a 5V USB charger.
    Second, please keep the ON-OFF power switch at OFF position and the LED indicator will show in red light, then the charging process gets started.
  • Does 5V/1A output work with iPad?
    Yes, iPad can be charged at 5V/1A , but with the charging time longer than that of 5V/2A
  • How to charge a mobile device ?
    When charging a mobile device, please follow the steps.
    First , please plug the USB connector into the “OUT” port of the power bank , and then the other connector into the mobile device.
    Second, please keep the ON-OFF power switch at ON position and the LED indicator will show in green light ,then the charging process gets started
  • Is there any other protection of FSP PB Series power banks?
    Yes. FSP PB Series power banks have a complete protection circuit, including short circuit protection ,over current protection, over charge protection and over discharge protection .
